Travel Guide Brasil!

Top most beautiful states to visit in Brasil

1.Rio de Janeiro

Rio de Janeiro has the most beautiful museums and aquarium of the country. Besides that, there are a lot of popular beaches and clubs to go. Rio is also well known for the statue of Christ the Redeemer.

    Places to Visit in Rio de Janeiro


  • CHRIST REDEEMER AND SUGAR BREAD MOUNTAIN
  • BOTANICAL GARDEN
  • TIJUCA FOREST
  • MUSEUM OF TOMORROW
  • SUNSET IN ARPOADOR AND LAPA AT NIGHT
  • FOOTSTEPS IN THE HISTORICAL CENTER

2.Minas Gerais

Minas Gerais has the most beautiful historic cities, with churches built in gold and old cities where the miners lived at the time of exploration. Minas still has the most beautiful waterfalls and natural beauty in the country.

    Places to Visit in Minas Gerais


  • Ouro Preto.
  • Belo Horizonte.
  • Capitólio
  • Poços de Caldas
  • Santa Bárbara

3.Bahia

Bahia has the most beautiful beaches of the country, with the most clean water and the best typical foods. There are a lot of historic touristc points too.

    Places to Visit in Bahia


  • Pelourinho: one of the most emblematic tourist spots in Bahia
  • Lacerda Elevator: the most famous panoramic view in Bahia
  • Mercado Modelo: compras e gastronomia em Salvador
  • Basilica of Senhor do Bonfim: colorful ribbons and festivities
  • TAMAR Project: marine life as one of the sights of Bahia
